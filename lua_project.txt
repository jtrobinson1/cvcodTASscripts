Automated the process to manipulate the RNG (with lua script) in order to obtain the desired drop items in the Low-Optimaztion Tool Assited Demonstration (LOTAD) of Castlevania: Curse of Darkness to increase the rate of which the frames could be checked to allow for a faster finishing time of the game.

Scripted a macro (in lua script) that would perform the optimized speedrun movement at its most basic level. Further manual control of direction was given by the PCSX2-v1.5-rr.v0.3 TAS (Tool Assisted Speedrun/Superplay) input tools. 

Macroed the process to exploit the infinite height gain glitch when used for skips requiring further than the normal vertical elevation.
